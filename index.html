Frequently asked questions

What are Google Local Service Ads?

Google Local Services Ads are a type of advertising that helps local service businesses connect with customers searching for their services on Google. These ads appear at the top of search results and include a Google guarantee badge to help build trust with potential customers.

How do Local Service Ads work?

Local Services Ads work by displaying your business at the top of Google search results when potential customers search for services you offer in your area. You only pay when a customer contacts you directly through the ad (via phone call or message), not for clicks or impressions.

What types of local service businesses are eligible for Local Service Ads?

Many local service businesses are eligible, including plumbers, electricians, locksmiths, HVAC technicians, cleaners, painters, roofers, and more. The available categories vary by location, so check Google's Local Services Ads website for the most up-to-date information in your area.

How much does Google Local Service Ads cost?

With Local Services Ads, you only pay when a potential customer contacts you through the ad. The cost per lead varies by industry and location. You set a weekly budget that determines how many leads you're likely to receive, and you can adjust this budget at any time.

How do I set up Google Local Service Ads?

Setting up Local Services Ads involves creating an account, completing your business profile, passing Google's screening process (which may include background checks), setting your budget and service area, and then launching your ads. The entire process can be completed online.
